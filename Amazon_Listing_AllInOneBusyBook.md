## SECTION 8 — PRICING & LAUNCH STRATEGY NOTES

> This is not a listing element, but these decisions directly impact how your listing performs.

### Recommended Launch Price: ₹499 – ₹599

| Rationale | Details |
|---|---|
| Competitor positioning | Most generic activity books on Amazon India: ₹150–350. Your premium positioning (24 themes, 135+ stickers, 300 GSM, free pen + duster) justifies a 50–70% price premium. |
| Perceived value | Anything under ₹400 will be perceived as "cheap" despite your quality. Anything over ₹700 without reviews will face hesitation. ₹499–599 is the sweet spot. |
| Launch discount | Offer a launch coupon of 10–15% (use Amazon's "Create a Coupon" feature) to drive initial velocity. This shows as a green badge in search results — significant CTR boost. |
| Bundle opportunity | Pen + Duster + Book as a bundle listing at ₹549 vs. ₹499 solo listing — both can exist simultaneously as separate ASINs. |

### Review Acceleration (Critical for Launch):
1. Enroll in **Amazon Vine** program (Brand Registry perk) — send 30 units to Vine reviewers pre-launch
2. Use **"Request a Review"** button in Seller Central for every order in the first 60 days (mandatory action)
3. Insert a **packaging card** (legal in India) that says: "Love the book? Share your experience!" — not asking for a 5-star review, just a prompt
4. Set up **Sponsored Products auto-campaign** from Day 1 (₹200/day minimum) to generate early keyword data
