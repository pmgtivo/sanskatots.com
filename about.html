Our Story

SanskaTots™ was born from one simple belief: Indian children deserve learning tools that match their curiosity, their culture, and their potential.

We saw parents buying activity book after activity book — each lasting just a few days before every page was used up. We saw toddlers gravitating toward screens because the physical alternatives weren't engaging enough. We saw Montessori principles being sold at premium prices that Indian families shouldn't have to pay.

So we built something different. Books with 300 GSM laminated pages that last years. Write-wipe-repeat technology that gives children infinite practice. Velcro activities that bring Montessori learning into any home. All at prices that respect Indian families.
