Questions Parents Ask

From what age can my child use this book? ▾
From 2 years onwards. The board book format is designed to survive toddler handling. The simple English explanations make shlokas accessible even to very young children. Reading together as a bedtime ritual works beautifully.
Which Gods are featured in this book? ▾
15 beloved Hindu Gods: Ganesha, Lakshmi, Saraswati, Shiva, Parvati, Vishnu, Hanuman, Krishna, Ram, Durga, Surya, Brahma, Kartikeya, Indra, and Balaji — each with their dedicated shloka and meaning explained.
