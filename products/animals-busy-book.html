Questions Parents Ask

From what age can my child use this book? ▾
From 1 year onwards with parental support. Independently from 2 years. The activities progress from simple animal identification to more complex concepts like animal homes and young ones.
Which animals are covered in this book? ▾
8 complete animal categories: Farm Animals, Domestic Animals, Wild Animals, Sea Animals, Birds, Bugs & Insects, Animals & Their Homes, and Animals & Their Young Ones — plus a Match the Animal Face activity.
How many times can each page be reused? ▾
The 300 GSM lamination is designed for hundreds of repeated uses. Under normal daily use by a toddler, pages will last years without degradation, ghosting, or residue.
Is the book aligned with school curriculum? ▾
Yes — activities are designed by Indian educators to meet what pre-schools expect children aged 2–4 to know before Class 1. Your child will be ahead of the curve.
Is this suitable as a gift? ▾
Absolutely — perfect for birthdays, baby showers, Diwali, and first day of school gifts for children aged 1–6 years. Beautiful and educational.
