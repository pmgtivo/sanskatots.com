Questions Parents Ask

From what age can my child use this book? ▾
From 1 year onwards with parental support. Independently from 2 years. The Velcro activities are great for younger toddlers, while tracing activities suit children aged 2–5.
Is the pen safe for toddlers? ▾
Yes. The pen is non-toxic and child-safe. The chunky barrel prevents choking and aids grip development. It's designed specifically for tiny 1–4 year old hands.
What if a Velcro piece goes missing? ▾
The book includes extra Velcro cutting pages. You can also use standard Velcro from any craft store as replacements — it works perfectly with the book's pages.
How many times can each page be reused? ▾
The 300 GSM lamination is designed for hundreds of repeated uses. Under normal daily use by a toddler, pages will last years without any degradation, ghosting, or residue.
Can this replace preschool? ▾
It covers all foundational concepts taught in playgroup and Nursery. It's an excellent complement to formal schooling and a complete alternative for home-learning families.
Is it suitable as a gift? ▾
Absolutely — one of the most complete, reusable gifts for birthdays, baby showers, and school admission celebrations for children aged 1–5. Premium quality that impresses.
